Drumming in AZ starts here!

DrumTimeAZ offers Hong Kong's best private lessons where kids and adults are equally welcome to learn the art of rhythm.
Whether your favorite drummer is Buddy Rich, Taylor Hawkins, John Bonham, Ringo Starr or Tito Puente - we can help you on your drumming journey.
We are experienced professional percussionists prepared to train drummers and percussionists of all levels - from the first day on the kit to advanced drummers with years of experience.
Our courses can be provided in English or French.

About Us

DrumTimeAZ ethos is enriching life through the joy of learning and playing drums within the context of the style of music that speaks to the student as an individual, ultimately leading to the ability to share this joy with others in everyday life.
DrumTimeAZ provides courses using state of the art equipment using a Roland V-Drums kit, one of the most advanced electronic drumsets on the market. This kit further approaches the feel and sound of an acoustic drum set more than ever before using tunable mesh drum heads instead of rubber pads. See for yourself online at www.roland.com/V-Drums
